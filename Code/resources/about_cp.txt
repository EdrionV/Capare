                                                    C.A.P.A.R.E is a historic fantasy text based or Multi User Dungeon (MUD) game.
                                                  The game starts around a time machine, where players could choose their characters
                                                and will be taken on an adventure based on where they get teleported to (period of time).
                                          Since every individual had/has or will have a strategy of ruling a state/kingdom according to their terms,
                                                          irrespective of what they do as profession or occupation in real life.
                                      The purpose of this game is to create a dominance for oneself over territories, based on their individual's skills.
